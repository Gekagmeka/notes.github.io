We initialized only with data attributes on <a> without JavaScript!!!

You can use
1) data-fancybox (image will be only opened - without swiping the other images)
2) data-fancybox="gallery" (other images will be swiped like a gallery)
3) data-caption="This image has a simple caption/name" (give name of the image)

Когда картинка не влазит, тогда отображается возможность приблизить (Zoom)
